## Section Descriptions

### Welcome & Setting the Stage (0:00–0:05)

A brief introduction to the workshop, learning objectives, and an overview of what we'll cover. We'll set expectations for the hands-on portions and answer any quick logistics questions before diving in.

### The AI Coding Landscape (0:05–0:20)

Where are AI coding assistants today? We'll survey the current tools, use cases, and why agents matter for modern development workflows. You'll understand the opportunity and see where this is heading.

### AI Subscriptions & Your License (0:20–0:30)

Practical guidance on access, subscriptions, and licensing. A handout covers the essentials so you can get set up before the hands-on portion. No surprises — just clarity on what you need to get started.

### Hands-on: Getting Set Up & First Interactions (0:30–0:40)

Hands-on time to get your environment ready. We'll step through the basics of connecting to an AI coding assistant and making your first successful interaction. Everyone works at their own pace; we're here to help if you get stuck.

### Prompt Engineering for Code (0:40–0:55)

How do you write prompts that actually get good code back? We'll explore patterns, common pitfalls, and techniques that work. Live examples show you exactly how to frame requests for clarity and results.

### AGENTS.md & Custom Instructions (0:55–1:05)

Meet the AGENTS.md format — a powerful way to give your AI assistant persistent, reusable instructions. We'll demo a real example and show how custom instructions unlock personalized workflows you can use every day.

### Hands-on: Write Your First AGENTS.md (1:05–1:20)

Your turn! You'll create a simple AGENTS.md file tailored to your own work. This is your chance to experiment and see how the format works in practice. We'll help you get it working and thinking about how to apply it in your projects.

### Skills & Reusable Extensions (1:20–1:30)

Beyond basic instructions, you can build composable skills and extensions that your AI assistant can learn and reuse. We'll show you what's possible and how to structure reusable building blocks for your team or yourself.

### Beyond VS Code: Terminal-Based Agents (1:30–1:45)

AI assistants aren't just for IDEs. We'll explore how to use agents directly in your terminal and shell scripts — powerful for automation, CI/CD integration, and workflows where you live in the command line.

### Sub-agents & Advanced Patterns (1:45–1:50)

A quick look at where advanced development goes: agents that delegate to other agents, multi-step workflows, and patterns for complex problem-solving. This is the frontier of AI-driven development.

### Live Demo (1:50–1:55)

An optional live demonstration or additional example, depending on where we are in the timeline. A chance to show you something unexpected or reinforce a key concept.

### Q&A and Next Steps (1:55–2:00)

Your questions, our answers. We'll also discuss how to keep learning, where to practice, and what resources are available after the workshop ends.

- Patterns for reusable skills and extensions
- How to use AI agents beyond the IDE — in the terminal and in automation
